2023 in music

This is my 20th annual roundup, a landmark befitting such a rewarding year in music. But it was a very long year, with hundreds of notable releases making it more challenging than ever to keep up.

First Direct Arena, Leeds. A fine time with the band that changed everything for me, eviscerating memories of a disappointing night at Ally Pally five years ago.
